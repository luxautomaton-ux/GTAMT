# GTA Money Team Fulfillment Checklist

## Template Orders
- Verify payment webhook.
- Unlock product in member account.
- Send setup email and download link.
- Create support follow-up task.

## Script Audits
- Collect resource list and server goals.
- Review licenses, webhooks, admin commands, and performance risks.
- Deliver audit report and fix priority roadmap.

## Server Builds
- Confirm QBCore or ESX.
- Create staging server.
- Configure database, Discord, firewall, and txAdmin notes.
- Record handoff video.

## Custom Routes
- Collect crew size, schedule, and target economy.
- Deliver legal route matrix and payout plan.

## Streaming Kits
- Deliver OBS scene collection, alert notes, delay setting, and platform checklist.
